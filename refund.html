Our Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with our services. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a refund within the first 24 hours of your service activation.

Eligibility for Refund

  • Request must be made within 24 hours of purchase

  • Refund applies to hosting services only
  • Domain registrations are non-refundable

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team through:

  • Discord Support
  • Email Support
  • Support Ticket System

Processing Time

Refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days after approval.
